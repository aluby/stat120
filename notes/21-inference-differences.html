CLT-based confidence interval for a difference in proportions:

\[\hat{p}_1 - \hat{p}_2 \pm z^* \times SE_{plug-in}\]

CLT-based test for a difference in proportions:

\(H_0: p_1 - p_2 = 0\)

\[ z = \frac{(\hat{p}_1 - \hat{p}_2) - 0}{SE_{pooled}}\]

What is the SE?

\[ SE_{\bar{x}_1 - \bar{x}_2} = \sqrt{\frac{\sigma_1^2}{n_1} + \frac{\sigma_2^2}{n_2}}\]

but, we don’t know \(\sigma_1\) or \(\sigma_2\), so we plug in \(s_1\) and \(s_2\) instead:

\[ SE_{\bar{x}_1 - \bar{x}_2} = \sqrt{\frac{s_1^2}{n_1} + \frac{s_2^2}{n_2}}\]
